B.I.O. D.E.M.I.S.E.

2024

This video displays a 500 page speculative graphic novel (8” x 8”). I created a imaginative narrative about humanity’s self-inflicted extinction by biological warfare. This project analyzes the collapse of our species and the emergence of a new world. A grim reflection on the enduring ecological consequences of human intervention, and how the world may continue without them. This long story allows me to ask the viewer to face the precariousness of our current environment and the ghostly inheritance we might leave behind.
