Design Practices

Fall 2023

For my Design Practices course, I was assigned a random utilitarian object to design a zine around, and my object was a cup. I created a satirical, 80 page book about the process of making cups, entirely designed in Adobe InDesign. The body text was personally handwritten and scanned into the layout, giving it a personal yet tactile feel. The final book measured 5.5 by 8.5 inches, was hand laminated, and spiral bound myself.
