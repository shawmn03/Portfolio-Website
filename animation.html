Stop-Motion Animation

Spring 2023

This stop-motion animation project was a visual storytelling collaboration piece. I co-directed and shot the practical footage with my partner and then completed the post-production process on my own. The project was a conscious exploration of advanced editing techniques. The emphasis was on dynamic pacing, and creative use of custom foley to create an immersive audio-visual experience.
